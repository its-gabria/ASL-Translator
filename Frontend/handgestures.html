Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the sign language recognition?
Our AI-powered system achieves over 95% accuracy with common sign language gestures. The accuracy improves continuously as our machine learning models are updated with new data.
Which sign languages are supported?
We currently support American Sign Language (ASL), British Sign Language (BSL), and are continuously adding support for more regional sign languages.
Do I need special hardware to use the translator?
No special hardware is required! Our system works with any standard webcam or mobile device camera. Just ensure you have good lighting for optimal results.
Is my video data stored or shared?
We prioritize your privacy. Video data is processed in real-time and not stored on our servers. All processing happens locally in your browser for maximum security.
Can I use this on mobile devices?
Yes! Our web app is fully responsive and works on smartphones and tablets. Pro users also get access to our dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android.
